HalalEats

What it does:

  • HalalEats is an Android app that allows vendors to sell home-cooked meals to customers in their city
  • Vendors publish their menus to let the customer know what can be ordered
  • All the customer has to do is search for the food they want, choose a vendor, then submit an order!

Technologies used:

  • Java
  • Firebase Realtime Database
  • Android Studio
  • Git
